Circuit Elements

The elements in the circuit and their attributes:

__CIRCUIT__

Circuit Checker

The circuit has to follow these conditions:

Condition 1: Circuit must begin and end with a tank.

Condition 2: Circuit can have only two tanks.

Condition 3: Second element must be a horizontal pipe.

Condition 4: There should only be one pump in the circuit.

Condition 5: All the pipes and bends should have the same diameter.

Condition 6: Succeeding pipes should have the same angle.

Condition 7: Bends should be preceeded and succeeded by pipes except before the final tank.

Condition 8: Pumps should be preceeded and succeeded by horizontal pipes.

Condition 9: Filters must be preceeded and succeeded by horizontal pipes.

Condition 10: Valve must be preceeded and succeeded by pipes of same angle.
